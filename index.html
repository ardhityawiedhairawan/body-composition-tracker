What does each parameter mean? / Apa arti setiap parameter?
  • BMI: Body Mass Index. Ratio of weight (kg) to height squared (m²).
    [translate:Indeks Massa Tubuh. Rasio berat badan (kg) dibagi tinggi badan kuadrat (m²).]
  • Body Fat (%): Percentage of your weight that is body fat.
    [translate:Persentase berat badan Anda yang merupakan lemak tubuh.]
  • Visceral Fat: Estimate of fat stored around your internal organs.
    [translate:Perkiraan jumlah lemak yang tersimpan di sekitar organ dalam.]
  • Body Age: Estimated age of your body composition, not actual age.
    [translate:Usia tubuh berdasarkan komposisi tubuh menurut alat, bukan usia sebenarnya.]
  • Subscut Whole Body (%): Fat under the skin as a percentage of total body weight.
    [translate:Lemak di bawah kulit sebagai persentase dari total berat badan.]
  • Subscut Trunk (%): Subcutaneous fat percentage in your trunk/torso.
    [translate:Persentase lemak di bawah kulit pada bagian tubuh/trunk.]
  • Subscut Arms (%): Percentage of fat in your arms.
    [translate:Persentase lemak pada lengan.]
  • Subscut Legs (%): Percentage of fat in your legs.
    [translate:Persentase lemak pada bagian kaki.]
  • Skeletal Muscle (%): Percentage of skeletal muscle in your body.
    [translate:Persentase otot rangka terhadap total berat badan.]
  • Skeletal Trunk (%): Skeletal muscle percentage in your trunk/torso.
    [translate:Persentase otot rangka di bagian tubuh/trunk.]
  • Skeletal Arms (%): Skeletal muscle percentage in your arms.
    [translate:Persentase otot rangka di lengan.]
  • Skeletal Legs (%): Skeletal muscle percentage in your legs.
    [translate:Persentase otot rangka di bagian kaki.]
  • BMR (kcal): Basal Metabolic Rate. Calories you burn at rest.
    [translate:Laju Metabolisme Basal. Jumlah kalori yang dibakar tubuh saat istirahat.]
